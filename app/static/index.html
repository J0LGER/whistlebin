What is WhistleBin?
WhistleBin helps you send secrets & confidential files securely by generating temporary links representing secrets, helping in tackling down data breaches attack surface!
When to use WhistleBin?
  • When you want to send secrets such as passwords through email or chatting platforms.
  • When you want to send confidential files through email or chatting platforms.
How to use WhistleBin?
  • For Secrets: Submit your secret and an expiration time, and just send the received link to your co-worker!
  • For Files: Submit your file and set an expiration time, a password-protected file will be instantly downloaded on your device, along with a the link that contains the secret password, send the new secured file and the link to your co-worker!
